SoNetAg

What Is It ?

Our product SoNetAg is a Social Network Aggregator tool which is available as an Android App and as a Web App. It is designed for collecting contents from multiple Social Network Services into one unified presentation. SoNetAg collates posts and updated from different social media feeds and provides an organized view.

For who ?

As our vision ‘Help the user see everything in one go’ suggests, the aim is to pull together information into a single location through consolidating multiple social networking profiles into one profile on your behalf. Any individuals having multiple social network accounts in different platforms, content creators and business organizations including E-commerce companies can use it in a meaningful and effective way to optimize time and keep track on existing feeds.

How you will be benefitted ?

Streamline your updates and postings through one source. Just create a single post and let SoNetAg do the rest for publishing in multiple social network platforms. Thus save your time and energy and never miss a possible entrance to reach your target group. This will result you to create an influential presence in social medias and thereby stand out from the competition with an extra edge. On top of it, social proof matters!
